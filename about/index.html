I’ve been homebrewing since 2010.

My primary focuses are flavorful low alcohol session beers, in-your-face hop-saturated IPAs, and all manner of mixed fermentation weirdness.

I’m from the Baltimore area and I confess to being a bit of a Sapwood Cellars fanboy. Thank you to Mike and Scott for all the inspiration.

This blog chronicles my brewing and beer related learning since late 2019.
