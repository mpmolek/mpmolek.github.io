About

2019-11-30

Hi, I’m Matt. This is a blog to document my homebrewing.

I like brewing a wide range of styles, but some of my focuses are flavorful low alcohol beers, IPAs, and lots of mixed fermentation weirdness.

I’m from the Baltimore area and I confess to being a bit of a Sapwood Cellars fanboy.
